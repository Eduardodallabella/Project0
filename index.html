About Upper

Project 0: Upper School

Upper is an English school, founded in 1999 that helps thousands and thousands of people to improve their English, and have a great experience when traveling abroad. We hope that the work our students do in English is enjoyed and strive to ensure that our curriculum offers a rich and diverse range of learning experiences. We are a team where each member enriches the department by their own contributions, strengths, and experience. It isthis sense of teamwork and willingness that enables us to face the challenges of teaching with confidence and enthusiasm.
